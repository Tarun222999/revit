Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Revit handles information when you use the Revit mobile app.

Revit is a personal entertainment journal. It lets you search for movies, series, and anime, add titles to your journal, track status, rate titles, write short reviews, and organize titles into mixed-media lists.

Information Revit Stores

Revit stores the profile details you create, including your display name, username, optional bio, and avatar path.

Revit stores your personal journal data, including saved titles, statuses, ratings, short reviews, spoiler flags, completion dates, custom lists, list items, and optional list notes.

Authentication And Account Data

Revit uses Supabase Auth for sign-in. Depending on the platform and sign-in option available to you, Supabase may process your email address and identity provider information from Google or Apple.

Authentication tokens are used to keep you signed in and to make sure your private journal, lists, and profile data are only available to your account.

Journal, Lists, And Reviews

Your journal entries, ratings, reviews, list names, list descriptions, and list notes are stored so Revit can show and manage your personal entertainment history.

Revit v1 does not include public profiles, social feeds, follows, comments, or likes.

Avatars And Storage

If you upload an avatar, the image is stored in Supabase Storage and linked from your profile.

Avatar files may be publicly accessible by direct URL so they can render in the app. Your private journal entries and lists are not made public by avatar storage.

Media Metadata

Revit uses TMDB-backed metadata for movies, series, and anime. This may include title names, images, release information, descriptions, genres, and related title details.

Your personal activity, ratings, reviews, and lists are stored separately from external media metadata.

How Data Is Used

Revit uses your data to provide the app experience, including authentication, profile display, search and title details, journal management, calendar and timeline views, and mixed-media lists.

Revit does not sell your personal journal data.

Ads, Analytics, And Tracking

Revit does not currently include ads.

Revit does not currently include analytics or crash reporting tools.

The public legal and support website is intended to be static and should not include tracking scripts, advertising scripts, login, forms, or database access.

Data Sharing

Revit uses service providers needed to operate the app, including Supabase for authentication, database storage, file storage, and Edge Functions.

Revit uses TMDB-backed integrations for entertainment metadata. Revit does not send your private journal entries, ratings, reviews, or lists to TMDB as part of normal app use.

Data Deletion

Revit supports in-app account deletion from the Profile account surface.

When account deletion succeeds, Revit removes the authenticated user account and user-owned profile, journal, list, list item, and avatar data.

For account or data deletion questions, contact mt790191@gmail.com.

Children

Revit is not designed for children under 13.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Revit changes. The Last updated date will be changed when this page is updated.

Contact
