Getting Help

Use the support email above for app issues, account questions, privacy questions, or data requests.

What To Include In A Support Request

When reporting an issue, include:

  • your device model
  • your platform, such as Android or iOS
  • your app version, if available
  • the screen where the issue happened
  • what you were trying to do
  • what happened instead

Account And Data Requests

For account access, profile, avatar, journal, list, or deletion issues, contact the support email above.

Revit also supports in-app account deletion from the Profile account surface.

Metadata Issues

If a title has incorrect poster art, description, release information, or genre data, the source metadata may need to be corrected through TMDB.

Revit can display metadata from TMDB-backed integrations, but it may not control the original source details.

Security Reminder

Do not send passwords, magic-link codes, OAuth tokens, private API keys, or other secrets in a support request.
